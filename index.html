What We Do

Double allows you to enjoy the benefits of having a responsive and capable assistant without the troubles and cost of hiring, training, and maintaining your own assistant staff.

For out-of-scope requests and ones requiring domain expertises, your Double will also work with third-party vendors to come up with the best solution and alternatives.

Regardless on the complexity of the request, your Double will always act on your best interest and coordinate the execution to ensure your requirements are met with the highest quality.
Here are the things we can help you with:
  • Manage your calendar and meeting schedules
  • Make reservations and arrange appointments
  • Gather information online and over the phone
  • Make phone calls for inquiries and customer services
  • Plan travel and purchase tickets
  • Manage your email inbox and social media
  • Organize events and manage invites
  • Purchase items online and offline
  • Sell your used items
  • Source, compare, and manage third-party vendors
Note:
  • A 5% payment handling fee applies to all purchases on behalf
  • Fees and charges from third-party vendors are quoted separately

How We Work

1: Send us your request

Start a conversation with your Double anytime.

We work well on both Slack and email. We recommend Slack for instant responses and email for bringing us in your external communications.

2: Work out the details

Your Double will clarify the requirement and send you an action plan with a time estimate.

Getting your requirements right is critical, that's why we insist on making communication with your Double free of charge.

3: Consider it done

Your request is fulfilled by our team of Doubles. You pay only for the minutes we used to execute our pre-agreed action plan.

We also work with partners on requests that require domain expertise.
